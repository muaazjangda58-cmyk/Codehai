Clothing & Wearing Accessories

Clothing is one of the basic needs of human beings. It protects our body from heat, cold, dust, and rain. Different clothes are worn in different seasons, such as cotton clothes in summer and woolen clothes in winter. Wearing clean and neat clothes keeps us healthy and presentable. Along with clothing, wearing accessories are also important. Accessories like watches, belts, caps, scarves, shoes, and socks complete our outfit. They improve our appearance and add beauty and confidence to our personality.
